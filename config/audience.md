# Audience

B2B Marketing Leadership team at a large global company:
- CMO
- VP Performance Marketing & SDRs
- Sr. Director of Brand
- Sr. Director of GTM
- Director Internal Comms
- VP CX & GTM Ops

These are experienced marketers, not technologists. They think in terms of campaigns, pipeline, brand equity, team capacity, and budget allocation — not architectures, frameworks, or model benchmarks.

They need to understand what AI developments mean for their strategy and budget decisions over the next 6-12 months.
