Meta's AI Now Runs Your Ad Campaigns
  • 65% of Meta advertisers now run Advantage+, the fully automated campaign suite — up from early-adopter territory to majority practice in under 12 months.
  • New in March: supply a URL and budget, AI handles creative generation, audience targeting, placement, and bid management end-to-end.
  • Audit which Meta controls you're still running manually — if the answer is "most of them," you're adding friction, not performance.

Cvent Buys ON24, B2B Event Data Converges
  • Cvent completed its acquisition of ON24, merging in-person, hybrid, virtual, and webinar management under one AI-enhanced platform.
  • The strategic play is first-party data: every event touchpoint — registration, attendance, post-session intent — feeding one connected scoring model.
  • If ON24 runs your webinar program, start contract conversations now — platform consolidations always bring pricing reviews, and the combined stack changes your integration assumptions.

Marketers and Buyers Disagree — By 40 Points
  • A new Charney Research/AMA study: 82% of marketers believe consumers benefit from AI-driven marketing — 42% of consumers agree.
  • Buried lead: 51% of marketers are already running campaigns targeting AI crawlers and models, not human audiences.
  • If half your peers are optimizing for bots, brands that invest in human-readable trust and clarity are building a durable, underpriced competitive edge.

Google Is Replacing Keywords with Intent Signals
  • Google's AI Max campaigns eliminate keyword targeting entirely — Gemini reads your landing pages and matches them to user intent automatically.
  • Shopping ads with Direct Offers now appear inside AI Mode responses; 1.5B users see sponsored results inline with AI-generated answers.
  • The new core skill for search isn't keyword strategy — it's landing page clarity and structured data, so the model can make accurate matches on your behalf.

Proving AI ROI: The Three-Part Framework
  • MarTech maps AI impact across three dimensions: time saved on production, output quality versus human baseline, and pipeline influenced by AI-generated content.
  • The common failure is tracking activity — automations built, content pieces produced — instead of outcomes the CFO can evaluate.
  • If your AI investment still lives in "we're experimenting," this framework is how you convert it into a board-ready ROI narrative before the next planning cycle.
