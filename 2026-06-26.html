Agencies Use AI to Cut Costs, Not Create
  • Forrester: 90% of US agencies use genAI, but 81% deploy it for productivity gains — not creative quality.
  • Top use cases are document summarization (74%) and competitive research (70%). Creative differentiation barely registers.
  • Ask your agency how they reinvest AI efficiency gains — in talent and better work, or just in their margins.
