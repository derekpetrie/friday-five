ChatGPT Now Sells Ads to Everyone
  • OpenAI launched a self-serve Ads Manager with CPC bidding — any business can now buy ads inside ChatGPT conversations.
  • Ads match on conversational context, not keywords; early advertisers include Adobe, Ford, and Target.

  • Google Marketing Live introduced Conversational Discovery ads and Highlighted Answers — formats built for AI Mode, not blue links.

Consumers Want Proof a Human Made It
  • Only 19% of consumers feel excited about AI — down from 50% two years ago.
  • iHeartMedia runs "guaranteed human" branding; others now commission behind-the-scenes proof that real people made the ad.
  • Use AI for targeting and operations, but keep a human byline on anything your audience actually reads.

Build the Brain Before the Pen
  • Ahrefs' content team built 16 AI tools in a hackathon; the ones organizing research outperformed every writing tool.
  • Knowledge bases and style guides fed to AI beat prompt engineering every time.
  • Invest in your team's memory layer — saved research, brand rules, past work — before buying another content generator.

Hershey Cut Mix Modeling to Monthly
  • Hershey replaced its legacy mix model with AI agents from Mutinex and Tracer across $2B+ in annual media spend.
  • Analysis that used to take months now runs monthly, with an expected 4–5% revenue lift from faster reallocation.
